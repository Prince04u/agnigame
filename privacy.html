AgniClub Privacy Policy

AgniClub respects the privacy of its community. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information AgniClub collects, why we collect it, how we use it, how long we keep it and the rights you have over it. The policy is written in plain language so you can understand it without a legal background. By using AgniClub you accept this policy.

What we collect

AgniClub collects only the information needed to operate the platform, meet legal obligations and keep the community safe. This typically includes the account details you provide when you sign up, the bank details you bind for payouts, technical information about the device and connection you use, and information about your activity inside the AgniClub application.

Why we collect it

  • To create and operate your AgniClub account
  • To process payouts securely
  • To detect and prevent fraud, cheating and abuse
  • To improve the platform and the player experience
  • To meet legal and regulatory obligations
  • To communicate with you about the platform when needed

How we use it

We use the information described above only for the purposes listed in this policy. We do not sell or trade personal information. Where we use third-party providers for technical infrastructure or operational support, those providers are bound by contracts that require them to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we instruct.

How long we keep it

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, including any legal retention requirements. When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or anonymise it.

How we protect it

AgniClub uses modern security practices to protect personal information, including transport encryption, access controls and continuous monitoring. No system is perfect, but we work hard to reduce risk and respond quickly if something does go wrong. The Trust & Security page covers our broader security approach.

Your rights

You have rights over the personal information AgniClub holds about you, including the right to access it, correct it and, in some cases, request its deletion. To exercise these rights please reach us through the channels listed on the Contact page.

Cookies and similar technologies

The AgniClub website uses minimal cookies and similar technologies that are necessary for the site and the application to function. We do not use intrusive tracking. Your browser settings let you control cookies if you wish.

Children

AgniClub is intended for adults only. We do not knowingly collect information from children, and accounts must be created by people who are of legal age in their jurisdiction.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date and, where appropriate, notify the community through the AgniClub application. Please review the policy from time to time to stay informed.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this policy or how your information is handled, please reach us through the official channels listed on the Contact page.
